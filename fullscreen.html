Investing In Your Health

When practicing yoga, it is important to remember that every body is different and to respect what your body needs. There are so many different styles of yoga out there that I know yoga can help you achieve your physical and spiritual goals.

Restorative Yoga

Restorative Yoga is a relaxing, meditative class that helps relieve mental and physical stress/tension. The mind and body relax while you slowly move through restful poses that are held for several minutes at a time. This is a great class for anyone who spends long hours standing or sitting at a desk, anyone experiencing chronic pain, as well as anyone who desires more rest and relaxation.

Partner Yoga

This type of practice cultivates physical and emotional support with your partner and helps develop trust in your partner as you learn to let go. Simultaneously, this practice helps develop self-awareness and trust in yourself.
